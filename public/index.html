How to play

Choose a mokepon.

Every mokepon has it's own element,
making them weaker or stronger against other mokepons.

Search for an oponent.

Move through the map in search of an oponent
to battle with. When 2 players collide, a battle starts.

BATTLE!

Do you know the rock-paper-scissors game?
Well, the battle works like 5 of them but with elements, in sequence and in secret.

You have to pick which attack you will go with first, then the second and so on.
When both players have selected all of his attacks, the selections will be revealed
and a winner will be chosen.
